About Dollmare

Dollmare is a haunting psychological horror game that blends elements of exploration, puzzle-solving, and narrative-driven gameplay. Set in a mysterious abandoned mansion, players must navigate through increasingly disturbing environments while uncovering the dark secrets hidden within.

The game features multiple endings based on your choices, immersive sound design that enhances the horrifying atmosphere, and unique mechanics that challenge your perception of reality.

Whether you're a horror enthusiast or new to the genre, Dollmare offers an unforgettable experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat from start to finish.
